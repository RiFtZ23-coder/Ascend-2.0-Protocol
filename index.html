Androgen Cascade
Cholesterol → DHT Pathway MODERATE
1Cholesterol → Pregnenolone — Enzyme: CYP11A1 (cholesterol side-chain cleavage). Cofactor: Vitamin D3 (VDR upregulates StAR transport).
2Pregnenolone → DHEA — Enzyme: CYP17A1 (17α-hydroxylase/17,20-lyase). Cofactor: Zinc (CYP17A1 zinc-dependent).
3DHEA → Testosterone — Enzyme: HSD17B3 (17β-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase). Cofactor: Boron (inhibits SHBG, increases free T).
4Testosterone → DHT — Enzyme: SRD5A2 (5α-reductase type 2). Cofactor: Zinc (SRD5A2 zinc-dependent). DHT = 3–5× more androgenic than T.
